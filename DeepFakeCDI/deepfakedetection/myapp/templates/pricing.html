What is DeepFake?
DeepFake is another threat introduced by AI. It involves the use of artificial intelligence to create hyper-realistic but entirely fake videos, images, or audio recordings. These can be used to impersonate individuals, spread misinformation, manipulate public opinion, or damage reputations. Detecting and countering DeepFakes is essential to ensure digital safety and maintain trust in media content.
